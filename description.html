Introduction and Background

Nepal has border shared with two giant nations India and china which have a strong defense mechanism in terms of human resource, technology, facility, and infrastructure and not to forget a huge economic backbone. Nations internal security is one of the vital aspect in determining the stability and prosperous of the country. Even though there are various mechanism and departments working around the clock to maintain harmony in the country there still remains a threat which appears sporadically from intrusion and various other forms which may compromised not only the security system but also in the economic activities of the country. Owing to Nepal varying geographical terrain and difficulty level of continuous monitoring by human personal it makes a sense to utilize the technology which can restlessly and continuously monitor the situation at the same time informs to the concerned authorities. Illegal trade, Human trafficking, Drug smuggling and many other activities may go unnoticed if proper measure is not taken. Early detection and tracking, Rapid action, timely response in real- time could prevent from huge loss in terms of economy, lives and Infrastructure.
